About Dr. Dumakude

Dr. Dumakude, also known as Siyamthanda Shange, is a renowned traditional healer based in South Africa. With a deep connection to ancestral wisdom, he offers guidance and healing to those seeking spiritual and physical well-being.

Over the years, Dr. Dumakude has shared valuable insights on various platforms, including discussions on health topics like strokes and spiritual matters such as 'Idlozi'. He actively engages with his audience through social media, providing educational content and raising awareness on important issues.

Dr. Dumakude is committed to preserving and promoting traditional healing practices, ensuring they remain relevant in today's world. His dedication to his craft and his community has made him a trusted figure in the field of traditional medicine.
